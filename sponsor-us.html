How does getting infront of over 200+ developers actively wanting to try new and different technology sound?

Hackference brings developers together to learn, build, share. On the Friday, developers share new tools and APIs in our two track conference. Then they come together again for a two-day hackathon to build cool new things with the technologies theylearned at the conference.

Sponsorship is your chance to get developers hands-on with you tool or API, both at the conference and at the hackathon. We have great value packages offering branding opportunities, booths at the conference and challenges featuring your tool/API at the hackathon.

The overview

  • Fifth edition of Hackference.
  • 200+ developers from the UK, across Europe and the USA.
  • Aimed at introducing fun tech that developers can use straight away.
  • Two track conference with talks on practical developer technologies.
  • Two day hackathon aimed at building fun new things with sponsors’ tech.
  • Branding, booth and hack challenge sponsorship opportunities.
